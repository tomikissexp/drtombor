ABOUT ME

At different stages of life and to varying degrees, but we all encounter situations that are difficult to handle alone or where we do not know how to achieve the desired change effectively. In such cases, most people turn to those closest to them ‐ family members or friends ‐ for advice. While well-intentioned, they often provide guidance as laypersons, which, at best, delays the resolution of the issue. Without an effective solution, many feel lost and ultimately as though they are left to face their problems alone.

My professional mission is to help as many people as possible realize and experience first-hand that seeking support from a professional for mental health challenges is not only a brave and conscious decision but also a completely normal part of life.

I earned my doctorate at one of the United Kingdom’s leading universities: University College London. I work with individual clients and, as an international expert consultant, I contribute to the development of behavioral change strategies for populations in European and Central Asian countries.

HOW I CAN HELP

I help you understand the situations, feelings and thoughts that create challenges in your life, and equip you with strategies to feel better as quickly as possible and navigate life’s difficulties more efficiently in the long term.

What would you like to change?

  • Anxiety and panic
  • Relationship problems and resolving conflicts
  • Coping, letting go of grievances and resolving losses
  • Self-esteem and assertiveness
  • Body image, weight and eating-related problems

WHY DO THERAPY

Maintaining dysfunctional life situations, emotional, thinking and behavioural patterns that hinder your personal growth is draining. The same problems come up, keeping you stuck, while the situation remains unchanged, even after a long time.

Working on your personal growth, changing emotional and thought patterns, or healing emotional wounds is also challenging. However, over time, these efforts pay off and gradually bring you closer to inner balance and harmony.

Whether with me or another professional, I encourage you to take the first step toward healing your emotional wounds and correcting the patterns that no longer serve you. It might not always be easy, but it will undoubtedly be worth it.
